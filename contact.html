About Joy in A Box

We believe that your home should be filled with joy. Joy in A Box creates beautiful, handcrafted home décor that brings warmth and happiness to every space. From custom-made furniture to cozy textiles, we're here to help you create a home you love.

Founded in 2024, we work with talented artisans who pour their hearts into every piece. Sustainability and quality are at the core of everything we do.

Our Values

  • Sustainable materials
  • Ethical production
  • Customer happiness guaranteed
